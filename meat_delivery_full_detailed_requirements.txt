MEAT DELIVERY PLATFORM - FULL DETAILED REQUIREMENT DOCUMENT
Swiggy/Blinkit Style Meat Delivery System

====================================================
1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
====================================================

Project Type:
Full-stack logistics-enabled meat delivery web application.

Goal:
Create a professional meat delivery platform where:
- Customers order meat online
- Riders deliver orders
- Admin manages operations
- Entire business can run through CRM/CMS

Target:
A complete handover-ready business system.

Core Concept:
A mini Swiggy/Blinkit for meat delivery.

Main Portals:
1. Customer Portal
2. Rider Portal
3. Admin CRM/CMS Portal

Must include:

- Offers applicable

Features:
- Add/remove quantity
- Update weight
- Add-ons

+ Taxes
